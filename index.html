We provide peace of mind

These are 3 most frequently asked questions that we get from our customers. They are all related to emergency situations that can be very stressful and we thought providing advice on how to get a situation under control can be quite useful. Please see below what to do if you come across any of these 3.

Gas leak?

Follow these steps:
  1. Open doors & windows
  2. Don't smoke, use naked flame or electrical appliances incl. lights
  3. Leave the property
  4. Then...
Call us on 07527 855 770
or 0800 111 999

Water leak from mains?

Follow these steps:
  1. Place a bucket under the leak if possible
  2. Turn off stop tap - usually found under kitchen sink, in downstairs bathroom or utility room
  3. Open all taps to drain remaining water from the pipes
  4. Then...
Call us on 07527 855 770

Leak from central heating?

Follow these steps:
  1. Place a bucket under the leak if possible
  2. Turn off stop tap - usually found under kitchen sink, in downstairs bathroom or utility room
  3. Turn off boiler and/or immersion heater
  4. Then...
Call us on 07527 855 770
